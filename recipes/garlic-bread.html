Garlic Bread

Cold weather calls for a cozy delicious bowl of hot lasagna soup. All the goodness thats fits in a bowl. Miss the sauce of a Lasagna, then this soup will not only be saucey but creamy too.

Ingerdients:

  • Lasagna Strips / or pasta of your choice
  • Herbs: Cilantro, Chives, Thymes
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper
  • 2-4 Green Chillies
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Zucchinis
  • 2 Gralic cloves minced
  • 1 Canned / Bottled Marinara Sauce
  • Feta Cheese
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Italian Seasoning>

Steps

  1. Get your Instant Pot out and hit the saut√© mode
  2. Add some oil then add Onions, Green Bell Peppers and Green Chillies, mix all together
  3. Add Italian Seasoning and the minced Gralic
  4. Add Zucchinis and Herbs
  5. Add a 2 cups water and the Lasagna Strips
  6. Put the lid on, hit the pressure cook option on the Instand Pot
  7. Pressure cook for 5-10 minutes
  8. Open the lid
  9. Add Marinara Sauce and Feta Cheese, mix well
  10. Serve and add some Chili Flakes
  11. Enjoy!
